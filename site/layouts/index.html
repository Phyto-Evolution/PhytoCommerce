What Is PhytoCommerce?

Generic e-commerce platforms were built to sell shoes and electronics. Plant businesses are different. You track TC batch provenance. You manage seasonal availability and live arrival windows. You deal with phytosanitary certificates, KYC checks for wholesale pricing, and customers who need to know exactly what growth stage their Nepenthes is at before they commit.

PhytoCommerce is a suite of modules — 28 and counting — that slots into the platforms your developers already know, and gives your plant store the specialist features it actually needs. No workarounds. No spreadsheets alongside your cart. Just a store that speaks plant.

"Built by plant people, for plant people — every module exists because a real nursery or TC lab needed it."

The first implementations are live on PrestaShop 8 and being ported to WooCommerce. OpenCart, BigCommerce, and a bespoke scratch-built stack are next in line — same feature set, idiomatic code for each platform.
