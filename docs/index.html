Questions

Do I need technical knowledge to run my store? ›
None at all. Your store comes fully configured — hosting, SSL certificate, payment setup, and all 16+ plant-specific features are ready on day one. If you can use Instagram, you can run a PhytoLabs store.
What happens if I miss a payment? ›
You get a 7-day grace period. Your store is paused — not deleted. If payment is resolved within 7 days, everything resumes exactly as it was. We hold your data for 30 days after cancellation before permanently removing it, and we'll email you a data export link first.
Do I need my own domain name? ›
No. The moment you sign up, your store gets a free address like yourstore.carnivorousplants.in — no domain purchase needed, no DNS configuration, nothing. If you already own a domain and want to use it (e.g. shop.yourdomain.com), custom domain mapping is on our roadmap and coming soon after launch.
